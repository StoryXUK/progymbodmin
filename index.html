More About Us

Unlock Your Full Potential, with Pro Gym Bodmin.

Welcome to Pro Gym Bodmin. your ultimate destination for achieving your fitness goals. We understand the importance of leading a healthy lifestyle and provide a top-notch fitness facility to support you in your fitness journey.

At Pro Gym Bodmin, our mission is to empower individuals to achieve their fitness goals through personalised guidance, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive community environment.

Our vision is to be the leading fitness destination in Bodmin, Cornwall, known for our commitment to excellence in equipment, training, and customer service, fostering a culture of health and wellness in our community.

To provide exceptional service with personalised programs, attentive staff, and a welcoming atmosphere, while continuously enhancing our facilities to meet evolving needs and fostering a supportive, inclusive community.
